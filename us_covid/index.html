For the seek more correlation wiht air quality and COVID-19 death rate, we analysis with EPA(Environmental Protection Agency) API(Air Pollution Index).

API(Air Pollution Index) : The Air Quality Index (AQI) is an index calculated for four major air pollutants regulated by the Clean Air Act: ground-level ozone, particle pollution, carbon monoxide, and sulfur dioxide.

AQI index shows more correlation with COVID-19 death rate. It means ozone, carbon monoxide and sulfur dioxide also impact on COVID-19 death rate. Thus, We need consistently monitoring and improving AQI factors.
