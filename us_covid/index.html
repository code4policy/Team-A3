Any other index for comparison?

For the seek more correlation wiht air quality and COVID-19 death rate, we analysis with EPA(Environmental Protection Agency) AQI(Air Quality Index).

API(Air Pollution Index) : The Air Quality Index (AQI) is an index calculated for four major air pollutants regulated by the Clean Air Act: ground-level ozone, particle pollution, carbon monoxide, and sulfur dioxide.

AQI index shows slightly more correlation with COVID-19 death rate.(R2=0.07483) It means ozone, carbon monoxide and sulfur dioxide also impact on COVID-19 death rate.

Why states view correlation is low?

One of the reason is air pollution monitor location.

The monitor centers are not equaly spread on states. It caused bias on state level analysis.

Thus, AQI factor and COVID-19 correlation provide limited information on states view.
